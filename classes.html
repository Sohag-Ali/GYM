Our Classes

Discover a diverse range of exhilarating classes at our gym designed to cater to all fitness levels and interests. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, our classes offer something for everyone.

Strength Training

Duration: 8–12 weeks

Frequency: 3–4 times/week

Time: 45–60 mins/session

Focus: Muscle strength, free weights & machines, structured rest between sets.

Flexibility & Mobility

Duration: Ongoing

Frequency: 2–3 times/week

Time: 30–45 mins/session

Focus: Flexibility, joint health, yoga/Pilates inspired low-impact movements.

HIIT

Duration: 6–8 weeks

Frequency: 2–3 times/week

Time: 20–30 mins/session

Focus: Intense cardio intervals, fast circuits, serious calorie burn.

Group Fitness

Duration: Ongoing

Frequency: 2–4 times/week

Time: 45–60 mins/session

Focus: Fun cardio, dance-based sessions in an energetic social setting.
